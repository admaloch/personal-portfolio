About Me:

My name is Davis Maloch and I am a self taught developer from Tallahassee Fl., specializing in front-end web development and responsive design practices. My background was initially in music and I graduated from Florida State University with a bachelor's degree in classical guitar. I am primarily a recording artist, multi instrumentalist and producer.

I have always had a fascination with technology and first learned about web development after talking to other musicicians who work in the field. It has since become a passion and I now have aspirations to become a full-stack web developer. Please look at my page for more information on my current skillset, recent projects and what i've got planned for the future.
